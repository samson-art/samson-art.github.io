The culture and search for quality have created a fascinating and at the same time elegant product. The hides are of leather, the soles of natural or treated leather. Our mission is to build comfortable shoes, which apart from the design could be also comfortable during the whole day. We have different quality models. The search for details and design has created the most elegant shoe of our classic line.


Our orthopedic shoes are designed and made, in order to improve the deambulation and comfort of people. Suitable shoes (major volume) can be used in association to a customized orthotic. In the case in which the shoe has to be used to correct a specific pathology, its use has to be controlled by a specific doctor. Leathers are natural and not treated with prohibited substances. In addition, thanks also to the work of our artisans, the various elements of leathers are innovative and of the latest generation.

Our team is qualified in the production of shoes with height increase. By studying the design particularly, we are able to produce models of shoes with +7 and +10 centimeter of increase. The footwear seems to be normal externally. At the same time, thanks to the insertion of internal wedge, it enables the man to be taller in a comfortable way, stretching the body and supporting the posture and back in an invisible way.

Our Company

It has been working in the speci c shoe sector since 1948, specializing during the years in the production of men’s classic shoes. In the last few years, apart from the orthopedic shoes, it has developed a new line of shoes with height increase (+7 cm. + 10 cm.). This has been perfect for people, who want to be taller. Our shoes are made and produced only with Italian materials.
